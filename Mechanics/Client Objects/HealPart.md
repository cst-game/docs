![[healPart.png]]Heals a specified amount of health. Can heal multiple times after a cooldown.
> [!attention]
> Only applies to the local player to prevent health update conflicts.

# Config
- `Cooldown`: How long the cooldown before healing again lasts. Default is `1`
- `Health`: How much health is healed after the cooldown is finished. Default is `25`
